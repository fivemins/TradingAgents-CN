### Key Caveats That Weaken The Hold Thesis
1. **Upside skew risk**: If 2C reform delivers >200bps of full-year 2025 margin expansion, consensus growth estimates could be revised up to 10%+, triggering a 10-15% valuation re-rating to 22x forward P/E
2. **Downside skew risk**: A prolonged consumer slowdown could cut high-end gifting demand by 10%+, driving annual growth below 5% and triggering a 15-20% de-rating to 16-17x forward P/E
3. **Sentiment asymmetry risk**: 100% institutional bullish coverage means any minor negative surprise (growth miss, policy tweak) will have outsized downside impact, with no low expectations to cushion price drops
4. **Past mistake guardrail**: Avoid anchoring to historical growth/multiples (a common error for both Moutai bulls and bears in prior cycles) – adjust expectations in line with real-time sequential growth and secondary price trends rather than decade-old performance patterns
