# Fundamental Analysis Report: Kweichow Moutai (600519.SS)
Report Date: 2025-03-20

## 1. Company Overview
600519.SS (Kweichow Moutai Co., Ltd.) is the world's leading premium baijiu (Chinese liquor) producer, with an unparalleled brand moat in China's high-end liquor market. Its core product, Feitian Moutai, is widely recognized as a premium consumer good and store of value, making it the bellwether of China's A-share consumer sector.

## 2. Latest Financial Performance Analysis
Based on the latest available 2025 quarterly reports, the company maintains strong overall fundamentals with moderating growth:
- **Growth Trend**: For the first three quarters of 2025, Kweichow Moutai achieved total operating revenue of ~130.9 billion RMB, representing a 6.32% year-over-year (YoY) growth; attributable net profit reached ~64.63 billion RMB, up 6.25% YoY. Growth moderated from 10.67% YoY in Q1 2025 and 9.16% YoY in H1 2025, mainly due to the high base effect from 2024 Q3 and ongoing channel inventory adjustment as part of the company's marketing reform.
- **Profitability**: The company's gross margin remained stable at 91.29% in 2025 Q3, almost unchanged from year-ago levels, confirming that its premium pricing power remains intact with no margin erosion from competition. Accumulated return on equity (ROE) for the first three quarters reached 24.64%, still among the highest in the global consumer staples sector, reflecting exceptional capital efficiency. Net operating cash flow per share hit 30.5 RMB, highlighting the company's extremely strong cash generation ability.
- **Balance Sheet & Liquidity**: Kweichow Moutai maintains an extremely conservative capital structure, with an asset-liability ratio of only 12.81% as of Q3 2025, far below the industry average. The company has maintained a net cash position (total cash holdings exceed interest-bearing debt) for decades, with a cash ratio of 1.47 (liquid assets cover 147% of current liabilities), meaning liquidity risk is negligible.

## 3. Institutional & Broker Consensus
In the past month (through 2025-03-20), 5 major brokerages released research reports on Kweichow Moutai: 4 rated the stock a "Buy", 1 rated "Overweight", with zero sell/underweight ratings. Key consensus views:
- Average 2025 full-year EPS forecast: ~71.8 RMB, corresponding to a forward P/E ratio of ~20.2x, which is below the stock's historical average valuation range of 22-25x, offering a moderate valuation safety margin.
- Most brokerages are positive on the company's ongoing 2C (direct-to-consumer) channel reform, which is expected to improve long-term profit margins and strengthen control over end product pricing.
- Consensus expects 10-12% long-term annual net profit growth for the company, supported by steady price increases and volume expansion in premium product lines.

## 4. Key Risk Factors
1. **Growth Moderation**: Q3 2025 growth slowed to 6% YoY, down from double-digit growth in previous years. If China's consumer recovery continues to lag expectations, near-term growth may disappoint markets.
2. **Valuation Sensitivity**: As a core long-term holding, Kweichow Moutai's valuation is sensitive to changes in market interest rates. A sustained rise in risk-free rates could lead to valuation contraction.
3. **Policy Risk: Potential adjustments to liquor consumption tax could impact net profit margins.
