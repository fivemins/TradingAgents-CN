# Fundamental Analysis Report for 300750.SZ (Ningde Times/CATL)
Report Date: 2025-03-20

## 1. Company Profile
300750.SZ (Ningde Times, CATL) is the global leading manufacturer of power lithium-ion batteries for new energy vehicles (NEVs) and energy storage systems, listed on China's ChiNext board. As the world's largest player by power battery installation volume, it serves major global automakers and energy project developers, with core competitive advantages in technology R&D, scale of production, and supply chain integration.

## 2. 2025 Financial Performance Analysis
Based on the latest available 2025 financial reports, CATL showed strong accelerating growth with improving profitability:
### Top-line Growth
- Full-year 2025 total operating revenue reached **CNY 423.7 billion**, up 17.04% year-over-year (YoY).
- Quarterly growth accelerated throughout 2025: Q1 (+6.18% YoY), H1 (+7.27% YoY), Q3 (+9.28% YoY), and full-year growth jumped to 17.04% as Q4 demand for NEVs and energy storage picked up sharply.

### Bottom-line Outperformance
- Full-year attributable net profit reached **CNY 72.2 billion**, growing 42.28% YoY – more than double the revenue growth rate.
- Deducted net profit (excluding non-recurring gains/losses) grew even faster at 43.37% YoY, reaching CNY 64.51 billion, indicating core business profitability is driving earnings growth.
- Earnings per share (EPS) for 2025 hit **CNY 16.14**, reflecting strong per-share profitability.

### Profitability Trend
Gross margin expanded steadily through 2025, from 24.41% in Q1 to 26.27% for the full year, driven by stabilized raw material (lithium carbonate) costs, product mix optimization (higher-margin energy storage and high-performance power batteries), and scale efficiency.
Return on Equity (ROE) reached 24.91% for the full year 2025, an extremely strong level for a large-cap manufacturing company, indicating high capital utilization efficiency.

### Cash Flow & Solvency
- Operating cash flow per share hit CNY 29.19 in 2025, far exceeding EPS, meaning earnings are of high quality with strong cash collection capability.
- Asset-liability ratio is 61.94% (2025 full year), a reasonable level for a capital-intensive industry. Current ratio (1.60) and quick ratio (1.36) are both above 1, indicating healthy short-term solvency and liquidity.

## 3. Institutional & Shareholder Sentiment
### Broker Research Consensus
In the most recent month (ending 2025-03-20), 9 brokerages published research reports on CATL:
- 4 out of 5 top reports rated "Buy", 1 rated "Overweight", showing an overwhelmingly bullish consensus.
- Consensus earnings forecasts expect EPS of ~CNY 20 in 2026 and ~CNY 24.5 in 2027, corresponding to forward P/E of ~18x (2026) and ~15x (2027), which is reasonable for a global industry leader with sustained high growth.

### Shareholder Concentration
Historically, CATL has shown increasing shareholder concentration since listing: total shareholder count decreased from ~368,700 at IPO to ~76,000 by 2019 Q1, reflecting sustained institutional holdings and long-term confidence in the company's growth trajectory.

## 4. Key Takeaways for Traders
CATL continues to outperform market expectations with accelerating revenue growth and expanding margins, driven by its leading position in both the fast-growing NEV power battery and energy storage markets. The strong balance sheet, high-quality cash flow, and bullish institutional consensus make it a core holding for growth-oriented investors.
