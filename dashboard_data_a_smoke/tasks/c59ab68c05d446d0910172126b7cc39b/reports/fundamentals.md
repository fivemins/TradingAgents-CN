## 1. Company Basic Profile
Kweichow Moutai is China's leading high-end baijiu (Chinese premium liquor) producer, listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange in August 2001. As of the analysis date:
- Current share price: RMB 1445.0 per share
- Total/Outstanding shares: ~1.252 billion (fully circulated)
- Total market capitalization: ~RMB 1.81 trillion, ranking among the top 5 largest consumer staples companies in the A-share market
- Core competitive advantage: Unmatched national brand equity, unrivaled pricing power in the domestic premium liquor segment.

### Key Financial Insights:
1. **Unmatched Profitability**: Moutai maintains a gross margin consistently above 91% — one of the highest among all A-share listed companies — reflecting its extremely strong brand moat and pricing power. Net margin also stays above 49%, showing industry-leading cost efficiency.
2. **Steady Moderation Growth**: After strong double-digit growth in 2024, Moutai's growth moderated to 6.32% YoY in the first three quarters of 2025, which is largely driven by a higher base effect from 2024. This stable positive growth still outperforms most peers in the baijiu sector amid the ongoing moderate consumption recovery in China.
3. **Extremely Strong Balance Sheet**: Moutai's debt-to-asset ratio remains below 20%, with very strong cash generation: net operating cash flow per share reached RMB 30.50 in the first three quarters of 2025, providing solid support for steady dividend payouts and future business expansion.
4. **High Shareholder Returns**: 2025 YTD ROE reaches 24.64%, far above the average for the consumer staples sector, indicating consistent high returns for shareholders.
