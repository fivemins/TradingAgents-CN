### Rationale
The buy case is overwhelmingly supported by verifiable, realized operational performance that outweighs mostly speculative forward risks. CATL’s track record of outperforming during industry headwinds (gaining share mid-price war, driving 1/3 of 2025 margin expansion via sustainable product mix shifts to high-margin energy storage and high-density batteries, not just temporary lithium price drops) confirms a durable competitive moat that is not fully priced into current valuations.

The safe analyst’s concerns about unpriced near-term risks are valid, but overstated: consensus 2026 margin projections already account for slower European demand, and CATL’s growing domestic and energy storage exposure reduces its reliance on volatile Western markets to mitigate geopolitical risk. The neutral analyst’s middle ground eliminates the flaws of both extreme positions: it avoids leaving most of the position on the sidelines to chase a marginal better entry, while also avoiding overexposure to near-term volatility. The asymmetric risk-reward remains highly attractive even with adjusted entry terms: maximum downside is capped at ~15% with the 220 CNY stop loss, while upside to the 380 CNY 12-month target is ~47% from current prices.
