# Fundamental Analysis Report: 600519.SS Kweichow Moutai
Report Date: 2025-03-20

## 1. Company Basic Profile
Kweichow Moutai (600519.SS) is China's leading producer of high-end baijiu (Chinese liquor), and the largest listed consumer company by market capitalization in the A-share market. Key basic metrics as of 2025-03-20:
- Current share price: RMB 1408.07
- Total market capitalization: ~RMB 1.76 trillion
- Total fully diluted outstanding shares: 1.252 billion, all shares are freely floating (no restricted shares)
- Industry: High-end baijiu (Chinese liquor)
- Listing date: August 27, 2001

Moutai has a centuries-old brand heritage, with unrivaled pricing power and brand loyalty in China's premium gifting and banqueting liquor market, widely recognized as a strategic national brand asset.

## 2. Financial Performance Analysis

### 2.2 Profitability & Margin
Moutai maintains industry-leading profitability that is unmatched in the global consumer sector:
- Gross margin has stayed extremely stable at **91.3-92.0%** across all recent periods, reflecting unchallenged pricing power and brand premium.
- Net profit margin consistently stays above 49-52%, one of the highest among large-cap A-shares.
- Return on Equity (ROE): 36.02% for 2024 full year, 24.64% cumulative for 2025 Q3 (annualized ~33%), far above average for consumer staples, indicating exceptional capital efficiency.
- Earnings Per Share (EPS): 68.64 RMB for 2024, 51.53 RMB cumulative for 2025 Q3, on track to hit ~72 RMB for full-year 2025, aligned with consensus analyst forecasts.

### 2.3 Balance Sheet & Cash Flow
Moutai has an exceptionally strong balance sheet with negligible risk:
- Asset-liability ratio is only **14-17%**, with almost no interest-bearing debt.
- Current ratio is above 5.7 across all periods, indicating extremely strong short-term liquidity to cover operating obligations.
- Operating cash flow per share reached 30.50 RMB for 2025 Q3 cumulative, meaning nearly all reported earnings are converted into free cash flow for shareholders.

## 3. Recent Broker Research Consensus
Across 5 recent broker reports published in March 2026 (the latest available close to current analysis date), consensus is overwhelmingly positive:
- 4 out of 5 reports rate the stock as "Buy", 1 rates it as "Overweight"
- Consensus EPS forecasts:
  - 2025: ~71.8 RMB, corresponding to a forward P/E of ~19.6x at current price
  - 2026: ~73.4 RMB, forward P/E ~19.2x
  - 2027: ~76.9 RMB, forward P/E ~18.3x
- Key bullish catalysts highlighted by analysts:
  1. Ongoing marketing reform and direct-to-consumer (2C) channel transformation, which will reduce channel volatility and improve long-term net margins
  2. Stable structural demand for high-end baijiu, with Moutai's brand moat remaining intact despite economic cyclicality
  3. Gradual capacity expansion and product mix optimization supporting mid-single digit long-term sustainable growth.

## 4. Key Insights for Traders
1. Moutai is the highest quality consumer staple asset in the A-share market, with unrivaled brand moat, consistent cash generation, and a rock-solid balance sheet, making it an ideal core holding for long-term portfolios.
2. Current valuation (19.6x 2025 forward P/E) is fully reasonable, it already prices in the moderated growth outlook, limiting downside risk during broad market corrections.
3. The 2C marketing transformation is a key underappreciated long-term catalyst, which could deliver upside earnings surprises if implementation progresses faster than current consensus expectations.
4. Near-term growth moderation is a widely known factor, so there is limited immediate catalyst for major near-term share price outperformance, but the stock offers attractive risk-reward for investors with a 1+ year horizon.
