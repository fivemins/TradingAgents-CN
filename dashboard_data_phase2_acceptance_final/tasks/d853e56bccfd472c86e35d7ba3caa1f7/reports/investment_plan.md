### Key Caveats That Weaken the Thesis
1. **Opportunity cost risk**: The stock is likely to trade in a tight 10.5-11.7 range for 3-6 months if no catalyst emerges, underperforming high-growth sectors like tech or consumer in a broad market rally.
2. **Dividend yield is not guaranteed**: The 30% payout ratio has only held during periods of positive earnings growth; a >5% 2026 earnings contraction would likely trigger a payout cut, removing the key downside buffer.
3. **Binary macro policy risk**: An unexpected large-scale nationwide property bailout could drive 20%+ upside overnight, while a new wave of property developer defaults could push the stock down 15%+ in 4 weeks, neither scenario is priced into current valuations.
4. **Historical precedent limits**: The current combination of sustained low interest rates and multi-year property sector overhang is unprecedented in recent Chinese banking history, so historical bank turnaround return patterns may not hold.
