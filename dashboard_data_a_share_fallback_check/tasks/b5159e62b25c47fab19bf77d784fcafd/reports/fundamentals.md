# Fundamental Analysis Report for 600519.SS (Kweichow Moutai)
Analysis Date: 2025-03-20

## 1. Company Profile
600519.SS (Kweichow Moutai) is China's leading high-end baijiu (Chinese liquor) producer, with a centuries-old iconic brand that commands unmatched pricing power and consumer loyalty in the global Chinese liquor market. As the largest large-cap consumer staple in A-share, it is widely held by domestic and international institutional investors, and acts as a key bellwether for China's high-end consumer spending trends.

## 2. Core Financial Performance (2024-2025)
Based on the latest available financial disclosures:
### Top-Line Growth Trend
Kweichow Moutai has delivered steady positive growth, with a sequential moderation in expansion pace in 2025:
- 2024 Full Year: Total operating revenue of RMB 174.14 billion, +15.66% YoY
- 2025 Q1: Revenue RMB 51.44 billion, +10.67% YoY
- 2025 H1: Cumulative revenue RMB 91.09 billion, +9.16% YoY
- 2025 Q3 YTD: Cumulative revenue RMB 130.90 billion, +6.32% YoY

The slowdown is driven by a high base effect from 2024 and ongoing channel restructuring (shifting from wholesale to direct 2C sales), which has temporarily muted wholesale channel growth, rather than weakening end demand.

### Profitability & Margins
Profitability remains extremely strong and stable, confirming the company's unchallenged pricing power:
- Gross margin has held steady between 91.29% and 91.97% across all 2025 reports, one of the highest gross margins among global consumer goods companies
- Net profit margin stayed at 52.08-54.89% in 2025, largely consistent with 52.27% in 2024 full year, with no margin compression
- ROE YTD 2025 Q3 is 24.64%, an extremely strong return on equity for a large-cap company
- 2025 Q3 YTD basic EPS is RMB 51.53, with attributable net profit of RMB 64.63 billion (+6.25% YoY), matching the top-line growth trend.

### Balance Sheet & Liquidity
The company maintains an industry-leading low-risk balance sheet:
- Asset-liability ratio is only 12.81% as of 2025 Q3, indicating very low financial leverage and negligible debt risk
- Operating cash flow per share is RMB 30.5 as of 2025 Q3, reflecting strong, stable cash generation from core business
- Net assets per share (BPS) is RMB 205.28, with steady book value growth over time.

## 3. Broker Research Consensus
Among 5 recent analyst reports, the overwhelming consensus is bullish:
- 4 out of 5 reports recommend **Buy**, 1 recommends **Overweight**
- Consensus 2025 full-year EPS forecast: RMB 71.07-72.55, corresponding to a forward P/E of ~20.0-20.5x, considered a reasonable valuation for the company's brand moat
- Key bullish catalysts: Ongoing marketing/channel reform (direct-to-consumer transformation) will improve long-term margin stability; the brand moat remains intact with resilient high-end demand; long-term growth from product portfolio and capacity expansion.

## 4. Key Insights for Traders
1. The 2025 growth slowdown is transitory, not structural: Stable margins confirm end demand remains strong, and channel restructuring will drive long-term profit improvement.
2. Valuation is attractive relative to historical levels: Current forward P/E (~20x) is below Moutai's 10-year historical average of 25-30x, offering a comfortable margin of safety for long-term investors.
3. Defensive characteristics: Extremely low leverage and strong cash flow make Moutai a reliable holding during market volatility and economic slowdowns.
4. Near-term catalysts: Further progress in channel transformation and potential product price adjustments could drive share price upside.
