## 1. Company Context
600519.SS (Kweichow Moutai) is the global leading high-end baijiu (Chinese liquor) manufacturer, with a decades-long brand moat and the largest market capitalization in the A-share consumer staples sector. It is widely held by both institutional and retail investors, making market sentiment and attention a key driver of short- to medium-term price volatility.

## 2. Recent Key Events Catalyzing Sentiment
Over the past week, two major events drove overall market attention and sentiment shifts:
1. **2024 Annual Earnings Preview (March 15, 2025)**: Moutai announced a 14.8% YoY increase in net profit, reaching ~75 billion RMB, which was slightly below market consensus expectations of 17-18% growth. This announcement triggered an immediate negative sentiment shock.
2. **Annual Distributor Conference (March 17, 2025)**: Moutai announced adjustments to its product and channel strategy: it will increase supply of its mid-to-high end variant "Moutai 1935" by 10% in 2025, and accelerate expansion of direct-to-consumer (D2C) channels to reach 25% of total sales by end-2025, up from 18% in 2024. This announcement partially offset earlier negative sentiment.
