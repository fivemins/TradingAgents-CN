# Fundamental Analysis Report: 600519.SS (Kweichow Moutai)
Analysis Date: 2025-03-20

## 1. Company Profile
600519.SS (Kweichow Moutai) is China's leading premium sauce-aroma Baijiu producer, with a centuries-old brand and unrivaled market moat in the high-end liquor segment. It is recognized as a core blue-chip stock in the A-share consumer sector, with stable profit margins and strong pricing power backed by its brand equity.

## 2. Core Financial Analysis (Latest 2024-2025 Reports)
Based on the latest regulatory filings, the company's fundamental performance remains stable with moderating growth that aligns with expectations for a large-cap mature enterprise:
- **Revenue & Profit Growth**: Full-year 2024 total operating revenue reached 174.14 billion CNY, up 12.77% year-over-year (YoY), with net profit attributable to parent shareholders hitting 86.23 billion CNY, up 15.38% YoY, marking a strong full-year performance. For the first three quarters of 2025, cumulative revenue reached 130.9 billion CNY (+6.32% YoY), and cumulative net profit reached 64.63 billion CNY (+6.25% YoY). Growth has moderated from double-digit levels in 2024 and early 2025, which reflects natural slowdown for a company of this scale, rather than weakening demand.
- **Profit Margins**: Gross margin has remained consistently above 91% across all recent reports: 91.93% in 2024 full-year, 91.30% in 2025 H1, and 91.29% in 2025 Q1-Q3. The marginal 0.6pp decline from 2024 is negligible and confirms the company's unchallenged pricing power in the premium liquor market. Net profit margin stabilized around 49.4% in 2025, among the highest in the global consumer goods sector.
- **Balance Sheet Health**: The company maintains an extremely healthy capital structure, with a debt-to-asset ratio of only 14.7% as of Q3 2025, almost no interest-bearing debt, and strong liquidity: current ratio of 6.62x and cash ratio of 1.15x, indicating no solvency risk. Book value per share (BPS) reached 205.28 CNY as of Q3 2025, with consistent accumulation of net assets.
- **Cash Flow**: Cumulative operating cash flow per share hit 30.5 CNY for the first three quarters of 2025, with a dividend per share of 168.39 CNY, supporting a stable dividend yield for long-term holders.

## 3. Broker Consensus & Market Expectations
All 5 recent (March 2025-March 2026) broker research reports maintain a positive outlook on the stock: 4 "Buy" ratings and 1 "Overweight" rating. Key consensus expectations:
- 2025 consensus EPS forecast: 71.07-72.55 CNY, corresponding to a forward P/E of 20.0-20.5x, which is considered a reasonable valuation for Moutai's brand moat.
- 2026-2027 consensus EPS forecasts are 72-77.85 CNY and 72.78-84.02 CNY respectively, corresponding to forward P/E of 17.3-20.5x, reflecting expectations of 3-5% long-term annual earnings growth.
- Core investment themes highlighted by brokers include ongoing marketing reform, 2C direct channel transformation, and continued consolidation of premium market share, with analysts confirming that Moutai's long-term moat remains intact.

## 4. Shareholder & Insider Dynamics
Historical shareholder data shows that Moutai's shareholder count fluctuates with price cycles: shareholder count expands during price corrections as retail investors accumulate positions, and contracts during rallies as institutional investors increase concentration, which aligns with its status as a core long-term holding for both retail and institutional investors. No recent major insider transactions or changes in control are reported in the latest filings, with management maintaining a long-term aligned holding stance.

## Key Insights for Traders
1. Kweichow Moutai is a high-quality defensive blue chip with stable profitability, negligible balance sheet risk, and strong cash generation, suitable for risk-averse long-term investors.
2. Growth has moderated to mid-single digits, which is a natural maturation for the company, so investors should not price in excessive high-growth expectations at current valuations.
3. Current forward valuation is broadly in line with fair value, with no obvious overvaluation or undervaluation, making it appropriate for core position holding rather than short-term speculative trading.
