### Key Caveats That Weaken the Thesis
1. **Mixed signal risk:** The 62.38 composite score is just below the 65 bullish threshold, so there is no clear directional edge, and either bull or bear case could play out depending on catalyst outcomes.
2. **Macro tail risk:** A sharper-than-expected slowdown in Chinese high-end consumer spending or corporate activity could cut gifting and event demand for Moutai faster than consensus models, leading to a deeper correction.
3. **Regulatory overhang risk:** While near-term tax compliance is confirmed, long-term risks of alcohol tax hikes or government curbs on high-end gifting remain unpriced and could trigger volatility at any time.
4. **Catalyst surprise risk:** A larger-than-expected Feitian price hike (≥10% lift) could trigger a confirmed breakout above 1600, invalidating the bearish resistance call; conversely, D2C channel conflict leading to distributor pushback could cause margins to contract faster than expected, invalidating the bullish margin expansion thesis.
