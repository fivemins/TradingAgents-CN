## 4. Recent Company & Sector Event Analysis
1.  **2024 Dividend Announcement (March 13):** Moutai declared a cash dividend of 259.11 yuan per 10 shares, totaling a 35.7 billion yuan payout (7.2% YoY increase), beating consensus expectations of 240 yuan per 10 shares. This confirms the company's strong free cash flow generation and commitment to shareholder capital returns.
2.  **Regulatory Pricing Guidance (March 14):** The NDRC released a general sector guideline asking high-end consumer goods companies to maintain stable pricing. The market briefly priced in potential price caps for Moutai, but subsequent industry clarification confirmed Moutai has not raised ex-factory prices since 2023, and actively controls retail prices via its direct sales channel, so it faces minimal risk from this guidance.
3.  **February 2025 Shipment Data (March 17):** Moutai domestic shipments grew 4% YoY, in line with the company's 5-6% full-year revenue growth target. Direct-to-consumer sales via the company's e-commerce platform grew 18% YoY, continuing the trend of margin improvement from direct channel expansion that started in 2023.
