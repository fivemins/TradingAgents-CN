### 3. Trading Impact
- **Near-term (1-3 months):** Continuous positive broker coverage of reform progress reinforces market confidence, which will support the stock price at current levels. Upside is likely as the market prices in better-than-expected margin improvement from 2C direct sales ahead of the upcoming Q1 2025 earnings release.
- **Medium-term (6-12 months):** The channel transformation is a material positive that strengthens Moutai's long-term moat: it reduces reliance on volatile wholesale channels, eliminates intermediate profit leakage, and improves the company's ability to maintain pricing stability. The current ~20x 2025 P/E is attractive for both long-term allocation and tactical holding, supporting moderate medium-term upside.
